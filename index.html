Which Neighborhood in Seattle is the Safest?

and how have crime rates been affected by COVID-19?

Data exploration by Cleo Tracey and Pei Lee Yap

Introduction

Many of us are newcomers to Seattle. We all have different priorities when searching for a new place to call home, and safety is a top one for many of us. So which neighborhoods in Seattle are the safest? We've taken a look at some of the most popular neighborhoods in Seattle, and we've visualized the reported crime incidents. In addition, given the emergence of COVID-19, we've used data visualization to explore how this pandemic may have impacted crime rates in Seattle. All data is taken from http://www.seattle.gov/police/information-and-data/.

Conclusion

Whether you're new to Seattle or just looking to learn more about your city, we hope these visualizations can help shed light on which areas in Seattle are safer than others. Based off the data on crime rates, it appears that the safest neighborhoods in our sample set are, in order, Magnolia, International District, and Roosevelt/Ravenna. At the end of the day, safety is just one of many factors that may influence where you choose to go, but we hope that this view provides you with additional insight.
